    What's New¶

    Version 0.1¶

    Released June 1, 2020

    This is the first major release of bar_chart_race adding many features:

    • Fixed bar position
    • Fixed max value
    • Perpendicular bars
    • Interpolation of the period
    • Formatting of the period label
    • Period label summary
    • Support for plotly colormaps

    Version 0.0.1¶

    Released April 29, 2020

    Genesis of bar_chart_race capable of producing smoothly transitioning bars with matplotlib and pandas.
